A Brief Introduction to BTLED

The Bachelor of Technology in Learning and Education (BTLED) program is designed to prepare future educators with a strong foundation in both technology and education. It combines the principles of pedagogy, curriculum design, and educational technology, equipping students with the skills to effectively teach and engage learners in a modern classroom. BTLED graduates are trained to: Develop innovative teaching strategies using digital tools and multimedia resources. Integrate technology into lesson planning and classroom management. Conduct action research to improve learning outcomes. Foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving among learners. With a focus on both practical classroom experience and technological proficiency, the BTLED program empowers students to become dynamic educators ready to face the challenges of 21st-century education.
